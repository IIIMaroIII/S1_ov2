**Idioms, Informal & Formal Slang Phrases (B2-C2):**

**I don't think we've been introduced.**
*   I don't think we've been introduced. | A polite way to initiate a conversation with someone you haven't met.
*   I - я, don't - не, think - думаю, we've - мы, been - были, introduced - представлены
*   Synonyms: I believe we haven't met; We haven't been formally introduced, have we?
*   Russian translation: Я не думаю, что нас представляли.
^^
**We haven't actually met, but...**
*   We haven't actually met, but... | A phrase used to start a conversation with someone you know of but haven't met in person.
*   We - мы, haven't - не, actually - на самом деле, met - встречались, but - но
*   Synonyms: We don't know each other, but...; I know of you, but we haven't met.
*   Russian translation: Мы на самом деле не встречались, но...
^^
**May I introduce myself?**
*   May I introduce myself? | A formal and polite way to introduce yourself to someone.
*   May - могу, I - я, introduce - представить, myself - себя
*   Synonyms: Allow me to introduce myself; Can I tell you who I am?
*   Russian translation: Могу я представиться?
^^
**I didn't catch your name.**
*   I didn't catch your name. | A polite phrase to use when you have forgotten or didn't hear someone's name.
*   I - я, didn't - не, catch - уловил, your - ваше, name - имя
*   Synonyms: I missed your name; Could you tell me your name again?
*   Russian translation: Я не расслышал вашего имени.
^^
**Am I saying it correctly?**
*   Am I saying it correctly? | A question to ask to ensure you are pronouncing something, like a name, the right way.
*   Am - (вспомогательный глагол), I - я, saying - говорю, it - это, correctly - правильно
*   Synonyms: Is that the correct pronunciation?; Have I pronounced that right?
*   Russian translation: Я правильно это произношу?
^^
**Long time no see!**
*   Long time no see! | An informal greeting used when you meet someone you haven't seen in a long time.
*   Long - долгое, time - время, no - не, see - видеть
*   Synonyms: It's been a while!; I haven't seen you in ages!
*   Russian translation: Давно не виделись!
^^
**I recognize you from...**
*   I recognize you from... | A phrase used to tell someone that you remember them from a particular place or event.
*   I - я, recognize - узнаю, you - вас, from - из
*   Synonyms: I know you from...; You look familiar from...
*   Russian translation: Я узнаю вас по...
^^
**Can you jog/refresh my memory?**
*   Can you jog/refresh my memory? | A polite way to ask someone to remind you of something you have forgotten.
*   Can - можете, you - вы, jog/refresh - освежить, my - мою, memory - память
*   Synonyms: Could you remind me?; Can you help me remember?
*   Russian translation: Не могли бы вы освежить мою память?
