**Vocabulary, Idioms, & Phrases (B2-C2):**

**Appropriate**
*   appropriate subjects | Suitable or proper in the circumstances.
*   appropriate - подходящие, subjects - темы
*   Synonyms: suitable topics, fitting subjects
*   Russian translation: Подходящие темы
^^
**Trivial**
*   too trivial | Of little value or importance.
*   too - слишком, trivial - тривиальный
*   Synonyms: insignificant, unimportant
*   Russian translation: Слишком тривиальный
^^
**Devolve**
*   devolve into an argument | To change from one thing to another, often to something worse.
*   devolve - перерастать, into - в, an - (артикль), argument - спор
*   Synonyms: degenerate into a dispute, deteriorate into a conflict
*   Russian translation: Перерасти в спор
^^
**Polarised**
*   polarise people | To cause something to divide into two opposing groups.
*   polarise - поляризовать, people - людей
*   Synonyms: divide opinion, create a schism
*   Russian translation: Поляризовать людей
^^
**Overlooked**
*   was overlooked | To fail to notice something.
*   was - был, overlooked - упущен из виду
*   Synonyms: was missed, went unnoticed
*   Russian translation: Был упущен из виду
^^
**Condescending**
*   being condescending | Behaving as though you are more important and more intelligent than others.
*   being - быть, condescending - снисходительным
*   Synonyms: patronizing, supercilious
*   Russian translation: Быть снисходительным
^^
**Balanced**
*   balance your life | To give equal attention to two or more things.
*   balance - сбалансировать, your - свою, life - жизнь
*   Synonyms: equalize priorities, find equilibrium
*   Russian translation: Сбалансировать свою жизнь
^^
**Engaged**
*   activities engage you | To occupy someone's attention.
*   activities - занятия, engage - вовлекают, you - вас
*   Synonyms: activities captivate you, pursuits absorb you
*   Russian translation: Занятия вовлекают вас
^^
**Pontificate**
*   pontificate about a subject | To give your opinion in a way that shows you think only your opinion is correct.
*   pontificate - разглагольствовать, about - о, a - (артикль), subject - предмете
*   Synonyms: hold forth, preach
*   Russian translation: Разглагольствовать о предмете
^^
**Conversational competence**
*   conversational competence | The ability to talk with others in an effective way.
*   conversational - разговорная, competence - компетентность
*   Synonyms: communication skills, discourse ability
*   Russian translation: Разговорная компетентность
^^
**Go with the flow**
*   go with the flow | To be relaxed and accept a situation, rather than trying to control it.
*   go - идти, with - с, the - (артикль), flow - потоком
*   Synonyms: be easy-going, take things as they come
*   Russian translation: Плыть по течению
^^
**Open-ended questions**
*   open-ended questions | Questions that cannot be answered with a simple "yes" or "no".
*   open-ended - открытые, questions - вопросы
*   Synonyms: broad questions, non-restrictive questions
*   Russian translation: Открытые вопросы
^^
**Multitask**
*   do you multitask? | To do more than one thing at the same time.
*   do - делаете, you - вы, multitask - многозадачность
*   Synonyms: perform multiple tasks, juggle activities
*   Russian translation: Вы делаете несколько дел одновременно?
